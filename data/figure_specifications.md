# Figure Generation Specifications

This document contains the author's detailed specifications for all PaperBanana-generated figures in "When the Middle Disappears." Each figure was generated using PaperBanana (Zhu et al., 2025), an author-directed academic figure generation tool analogous to Figma or Adobe Illustrator. The author specified the communicative intent, content, layout, and data mappings in natural language; PaperBanana generated the visual output through its plan-generate-critique pipeline. The author reviewed each iteration and approved the final versions.

All specifications below are the author's original inputs. The planner, stylist, visualizer, and critic prompts (generated by PaperBanana's pipeline) are preserved in the respective `outputs/run_*/prompts/` directories.

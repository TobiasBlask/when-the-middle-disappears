## 4. PREPRINT POLICY — FULLY PERMITTED ✅

### Can you upload to SSRN, ResearchGate, arXiv, etc.?

**YES — uneingeschränkt.** Elsevier's sharing policy explicitly permits:

- **Before submission:** Upload anywhere (SSRN, arXiv, ResearchGate, institutional repository, personal website).
- **During review:** You may continue to share the preprint. This does NOT count as prior publication.
- **After acceptance:** You may keep the preprint version online. The accepted manuscript (post-peer-review) can be shared after an embargo period (typically 24 months for Elsevier social science journals via Green OA).
- **Elsevier even offers automated SSRN posting** for submitted manuscripts.

### Recommended Preprint Strategy

| Platform | Recommended? | Notes |
